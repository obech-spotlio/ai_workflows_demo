---
name: jira-intake
description: Read a Jira ticket and turn it into implementation-ready acceptance criteria and constraints.
---

## What this skill does

- Read ticket title, description, subtasks, links, and acceptance criteria.
- Extract implementation scope, assumptions, risks, and missing details.
- Convert product language into an engineering checklist.

## Output format

- Ticket summary
- Acceptance criteria checklist
- Constraints and dependencies
- Risks or open questions
- Suggested branch slug
